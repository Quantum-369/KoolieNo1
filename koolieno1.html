About Koolie No:1

"Koolie No:1" is a platform designed to connect farmers and workers seamlessly. Our mission is to alleviate labor shortages in agriculture by providing an easy-to-use app where farmers can find skilled workers, and workers can find meaningful employment.

Features

Easy Registration

Create a profile quickly and start connecting.

Job Listings

Farmers can post jobs with all necessary details.

Search & Notifications

Workers can search for jobs or get notified about new opportunities.

In-App Communication

Contact potential employers or workers directly through the app.

Language Support

Available in multiple local languages for wider accessibility.

Rating System

Build trust through user ratings and feedback.

How It Works

Step 1

Download "Koolie No:1" from the app store.

Step 2

Create your profile as a farmer or a worker.

Step 3

Post a job or search for available work in your area.

Step 4

Connect directly and start working together.
